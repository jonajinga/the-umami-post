title: "Emulsify"
description: "Bind two ingredients that normally separate (oil and water are the canonical pair) into a single stable mixture. Mayonnaise, vinaigrette, hollandaise, and a finished pan sauce are all emulsions. The trick is usually a third ingredient -- mustard, egg yolk, lecithin -- and slow, steady mixing."
